## Senior Software Engineer | Backend Architect | DevOps & Platform Engineering | Drupal & WordPress Expert

Hello, I’m **Juan José Gómez López**. I am a Senior Software Engineer and Backend Architect based in **Cuernavaca, Mexico**, dedicated to designing, scaling, and optimizing complex enterprise web platforms for global organizations. 

Welcome to my personal slice of the web (which, keeping things fast and lightweight, is built statically with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/)).

### Building for Scale and Resilience

With over **9 years of full-stack development experience**, my career has been defined by turning intricate business requirements into high-availability digital infrastructures. I specialize deeply in the PHP ecosystem, particularly in architecting enterprise solutions using **Drupal (versions 7 through 11)** and **WordPress**. 

I have a proven track record of designing multisite architectures and high-availability systems. Whether it’s executing **zero-downtime migrations** for tens of thousands of users, or building complex synchronization systems that process massive transactional queues, my goal is always to deliver secure, scalable, and resilient platforms.

### The Engineering Philosophy: From Enterprise to Micro-Frameworks

My background in electronics instilled in me a fundamental appreciation for how things work "under the hood." This drives my approach to software architecture: **choose the right tool for the job**. 

While my day-to-day often involves heavy-duty enterprise environments and cloud infrastructures (AWS, Acquia, Pantheon), I am equally passionate about minimalism in code. When the project calls for it, I love building with lightweight tools like **FlightPHP** and **SQLite** to create highly efficient, resource-conscious software.

### DevOps, Automation, and the Future

An application is only as good as the infrastructure it runs on. A significant part of my focus is on **platform engineering, system integration, and DevOps**. I am continuously modernizing legacy deployment workflows, leveraging **Docker** and transitioning to modern self-hosted deployment platforms like **Dokploy** to establish true cloud-native infrastructure.

Beyond traditional web architecture, I actively explore the intersection of backend engineering and **AI**. I regularly utilize machine learning libraries like **MLX** and run models like **LLaMA** and **Qwen** locally to automate tasks and accelerate my development workflows.

### Let's Connect

I am always looking toward the next architectural challenge and strongly prefer collaborating with international teams in remote environments. 

Whether you want to discuss enterprise CMS architecture, the nuances of local LLM deployment, or just share a great cup of coffee (preferably espresso with perfectly textured micro-foam), I’d love to hear from you.
